EcoExchange

Transforming the world through recycling and sustainability

About

EcoExchange is a recycling-based platform for buying and selling recycled products. Our goal is to promote sustainability and create a circular economy. We connect buyers and sellers in a user-friendly marketplace. Join us to access affordable, high-quality recycled products that are good for the environment and your wallet.

How it Works

Buyers

Buyers can browse through our marketplace to find recycled products that they are interested in. They can then add these products to their cart and checkout. Buyers can also view their order history and leave reviews for sellers.

Sellers

Sellers can post their recycled products on our marketplace. They can also view their order history and reviews from buyers. Sellers can also edit and delete their products.
